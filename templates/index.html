What We Do

Plan Selection Assistance

Customer care representatives can guide you through the available health insurance plans offered by the company. They can explain the differences between plans. Based on your needs and budget, they can help you choose a plan that best suits you and your family.

Policy Explanation

Health insurance policies can various terms and conditions. Customer care can provide detailed explanations of what is covered under your policy, including medical services, specialist visits, hospital stays, and more. They can also clarify any exclusions or limitations in coverage.

Cost Estimation

Customer care can help you estimate the costs associated with your health insurance plan, including monthly and yearly coinsurance. They can provide personalized cost estimates based on your specific healthcare needs, helping you budget effectively for healthcare expenses.

About Us

Detecting fraud in medical insurance claims is like having a smart system that keeps an eye on all the claims coming in. It looks for anything fishy, like too many claims for the same thing or strange billing patterns. This system uses fancy math and learning from past cases to figure out which claims might be scams. If it spots something suspicious, it flags it for humans to check. This helps insurance companies save money by not paying out for fake claims and makes sure honest folks get their claims processed fairly and quickly.
